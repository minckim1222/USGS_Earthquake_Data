USGS Earthquake Data

A look at earthquakes around the world in the past 7 days

A graphical representation of earthquakes all around the world for the past 7 days.

The data is scraped from the USGS Earthquakes Website. The data is comprised of earthquakes of all magnitudes. The map is blank by default. There are control options for a bubble plot, a tectonic plate layer, and a heat layer. The bubble plot size and heat map color are based on earthquake magnitude.
